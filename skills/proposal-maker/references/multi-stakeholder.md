# Multi-Stakeholder Proposals

When the proposal will be seen by multiple decision makers:

- **CEO:** Lead with vision and market opportunity. ROI section is critical.
- **CFO:** Lead with pricing justification and benchmarks. ROI math must be airtight.
- **Technical lead:** Lead with deliverables and quick wins. Show you understand their stack.

You don't need separate pages. Weight the sections accordingly and ensure each stakeholder finds their priority within the first scroll.
